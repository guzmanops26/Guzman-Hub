Guzman Hub

Private financial infrastructure

What this is

Guzman Hub is a personal-use financial dashboard built and operated by its sole owner. It aggregates the owner's own bank, savings, and credit accounts into a single private database to track spending, cash flow, subscriptions, and debt over time.

What it is not

It is not a product or service. It has no customers, no sign-ups, and no users other than its owner. It cannot move money — all financial data access is read-only.

How it handles data

  • Bank connections are made through Plaid, with the owner's explicit authorization per institution.
  • All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256).
  • Application access requires passkey authentication — no passwords exist on the system.
  • No data is ever sold or shared with any third party.
